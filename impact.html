Impacts of Mis/Disinformation

Health Information

  • Many disinformation about health transmit through instant message application
  • People may believe in this disinformation, and use medical products that is not useful, even harmful to their health

Election

  • Misinformation about candidates are common during election
  • Being in a media environment full of misinformation, voters might easily judge a candidate base of these misinformation
  • Ultimately affect their voting decisions, and thus harm the democracy
